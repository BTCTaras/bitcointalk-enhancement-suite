BitcoinTalk Enhancement Suite Options
This is the options page for the BitcoinTalk Enhancement Suite extension. Note that the options you select here will be saved to your browser and not bound to your account.
Show newbies with silver rank:
Makes the coin rank under a newbie's name silver to easily distinguish them from jr. members
Delete newbie warning from reply:
Removes the warning from your quotes of newbies in DM so that it does not confuse/scare them
Show DefaultTrust badges on posts:
Shows a badge on the side of posts by users in DefaultTrust depth 1 or 2
Show DefaultTrust badges on threads:
Shows a badge on the names of users in DefaultTrust depth 1 or 2 in the thread list
Show account pricer link on accounts:
Adds a link to achow's account pricer to posts in some boards: Invites & Accounts, Lending, Long-term offers, and Meta
Show legacy scammer tags:
Shows SCAMMER tags assigned by administration prior to the existence of the trust system
Show scammer tags on negtrust accounts:
Shows a SCAMMER tag on any account with -10 or less trust score
Distinguish users on SMAS blacklist:
Replaces the rank image of users on the SMAS blacklist with five black X icons for high visibility
Show block explorer links:
Shows links to view addresses, transactions, and blocks in a block explorer as they appear in posts
Block explorer address URL:
The URL that links to a page about an address on a block explorer
Block explorer transaction URL:
The URL that links to a page about a transaction on a block explorer
Block explorer block URL:
The URL that links to a page about a block on a block explorer
